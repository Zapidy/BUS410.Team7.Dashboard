# Round 5 Showcase Dashboard — Design Context

## Users

Primary audience: the BUS410 professor and classmates during the final live presentation. Viewing on a classroom projector at roughly 10-foot distance. Five minutes to grok the headline finding, then reward closer interaction during Q&A.

Secondary: portfolio artifact afterward, viewed on a laptop.

Job to be done: convince a quantitatively-literate viewer that the model's tract-level desert-formation forecast is methodologically defensible, with the rigor visible in the surface itself.

## Brand Personality

Three words: **dense, clinical, authoritative.**

The voice of a Bloomberg Terminal screenshot, a Federal Reserve research tool, a TradingView pro-mode chart. Authority over warmth. Numbers over chrome. The interface signals "this is research-grade work" through what it withholds, not what it adds.

Emotional goals: trust through density, calm through alignment, mild reverence for the data itself.

## Aesthetic Direction

- **Theme**: dark. Required for the institutional register and consistent with the quant-terminal anti-references list.
- **References**: Bloomberg Terminal, TradingView pro mode, Federal Reserve research tools, Linear's command palette aesthetic, Datadog/Grafana when stripped of the chartjunk.
- **Anti-references** (explicit user-confirmed bans):
  - Civic-infographic register (Urban Institute / Pew) — what this is replacing
  - Generic SaaS dashboard — cards with rounded corners and drop shadows on a gray background
  - Glassmorphism / glow / gradient-heavy modern web — blurred translucent panels, neon accents, purple-to-blue gradients
- **Choropleth ramp**: single-hue sequential, dim → saturated. Risk dim at low values almost matches the surface; bright amber/burnt-orange at high values pops without being a "danger red." Avoid Viridis / Plasma / any rainbow.
- **Typography**:
  - Display: **Funnel Display** (Pangram Pangram, OFL) — geometric grotesque with slight quirk; not on the AI-default reflex list.
  - Body: **Funnel Sans** companion.
  - Tabular numbers: **JetBrains Mono** (free, OFL) with `font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums`. All numeric data uses it. Never proportional digits in data tables.
  - Aspirational paid alternative: **Söhne** (Klim) for display + body if a license is available.
  - **Reflex defaults explicitly banned**: Inter, DM Sans, Plus Jakarta, Fraunces, Newsreader, Instrument *, Outfit, IBM Plex *, Space *, Cormorant, Crimson, Playfair, Lora, Syne.
- **Color palette** (OKLCH-defined; tinted toward a cool neutral so accent reads warm against it):
  - background: `oklch(0.18 0.005 240)` — near-black, slight cool tint
  - surface-1 (panels): `oklch(0.22 0.005 240)`
  - surface-2 (elevated): `oklch(0.26 0.008 240)`
  - rule: `oklch(0.34 0.008 240)` — hairline, 1px, exactly at border-of-visibility
  - text-primary: `oklch(0.96 0.003 240)`
  - text-secondary: `oklch(0.72 0.005 240)`
  - text-muted: `oklch(0.52 0.005 240)`
  - accent (used sparingly): `oklch(0.78 0.18 65)` — burnt amber. The single warm color in the system.
- **Motion**: minimal and deliberate. Toggle changes crossfade ~250ms ease-out-quart. Hover ring appears in 80ms. No bounce, no elastic, no decorative micro-interactions.

## Design Principles

1. **Density is honesty.** A research artifact shows what matters, not what's prettily padded. Negative space serves alignment, not breathing room. The dashboard packs more onto a screen than a SaaS dashboard would, on purpose.
2. **Hairlines, not boxes.** No cards-with-shadows. Sections separate via 1-pixel rules at exactly OKLCH lightness 0.34. Panels float over the map without rounded corners or drop shadows.
3. **One accent color.** Used only for: highest-risk tracts on the map, the active horizon toggle state, the headline AUC/AP-lift number, focus rings. Everywhere else: neutral. Accents work because they're rare.
4. **Tabular figures, always.** All numeric data is monospace with tabular-nums. Numbers right-align in tables. The eye traces digit-place down a column without saccade jitter.
5. **The map is the page.** Not a card embedded in chrome. Full-bleed dark canvas; control panels and data tables float above it as opaque hairline-bordered overlays.
6. **Reads at 10 feet AND at 18 inches.** Same interface works at projector distance and laptop distance. Headlines + map color signal at distance; tabular detail rewards close inspection. No mode switch.
